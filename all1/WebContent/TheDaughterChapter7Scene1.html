Soon a wedding was arranged between the master's on and Cap o' Rushes. People from all over were invited to the wedding feast, including Cap o' Rushes own father.
Before the feast, Cap o' Rushes went into the kitchen and told the cook to put no salt in any of the dishes she prepared for the feast. 'But that will make the food taste horrible,' protested the cook. 'Never mind,' said Cap o' Rushes. 'Just do as I say.'
