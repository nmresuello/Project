As soon as the other servants had left, and the house was empty, Cap o' Rushes threw off her cloak and hood of rushes, and dressed in her fine clothes, went to the dance.
There she was the most beautiful girl of all.
The son of her master was at the dance as well.
He could not take his eyes off the girl, and danced with her all evening.
